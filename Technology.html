Precision Engineering

Discover the innovative technology behind every Aura timepiece

Swiss Automatic Movement

Our watches are powered by ultra-precise mechanical movements, crafted in Switzerland and accurate to ±5 seconds per day. Each movement contains over 200 meticulously assembled components.

Scratch-Resistant Sapphire

Featuring synthetic sapphire crystal with anti-reflective coating, our watch faces offer exceptional clarity and durability that resists scratches from daily wear.

200M Water Resistance

Engineered for professional divers with reinforced seals, screw-down crowns, and corrosion-resistant 316L stainless steel cases that withstand pressures at 200 meters depth.

Additional Technical Specifications

Extended Power Reserve

Our automatic movements feature a 72-hour power reserve, ensuring accurate timekeeping even when not worn for several days.

Magnetic Resistance

Protected against magnetic fields up to 4,800 A/m, making our watches resistant to everyday magnetic interference from electronics.

Luminescent Markers

Super-LumiNova® coated hands and markers provide exceptional low-light visibility that lasts up to 8 hours in complete darkness.

Ceramic Bezels

High-tech ceramic bezels offer superior scratch resistance and maintain their color and finish indefinitely.
